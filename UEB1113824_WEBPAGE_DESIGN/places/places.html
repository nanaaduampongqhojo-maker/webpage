Top Places in Ghana

From Historic castles to prestine beaches, discover the incredible places that makes
Ghana a unique destination

Central Region

CAPE COAST CASTLE

A historic coastal fortress built by European traders in the 17th century.
It later became one of the most important centers during the transatlantic slave trade.
Today, it serves as a museum and memorial, attracting visitors from around the world to learn about its history and cultural significance

Central Region

KAKUM NATIONAL PARK

One of the most famous tourist attractions in Ghana, located about 30 km north of Cape Coast in the Central Region.
The park is covered with dense tropical rainforest and is well known for its canopy walkway,
a series of suspension bridges high above the forest floor.

Greater Accra Region

INDEPENDENCE SQUARE

Independence Square is a large ceremonial ground built to commemorate Ghana’s independence in 1957.
It features the iconic Black Star Gate, the Independence Arch and a vast open space used for national events, parades and celebrations.
The Square stands as a powerful symbol of freedom, unity and national pride in Ghana

Northern Region

MOLE NATIONAL PARK

Known for its vast savannah landscapes, scenic hills and diverse wildlife,
Mole National Park is home to range of animals including elephants, baboons and various bird species.
Visitors can enjoy guided walking safaris and jeep tours, offering a unique opportunity to see animals up close in their natural habitat.
The park also features beautiful viewpoints and a peaceful environment, making it a top destination for eco-tourism and wildlfie adventures in Ghana

Bono East Region

KINTAMPO WATERFALLS

A scenic cascade flowing over rocky steps surrounded by lush forest vegetation.
The waterfall drops from a height of about 70 meters, creating a cool and refreshing atmosphere for visitors.
It is a popular spot for relaxation, picnics and relaxation and visitors can walk down a set of stairs to enjoy the view from the base.
The site offers a peaceful natural setting, making a favorite destination for both tourists and locals.
